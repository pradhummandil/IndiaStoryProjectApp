The Scholarly Mandate

The Vittala Temple complex at Hampi stands as the pinnacle of Vijayanagara architecture. Its centerpiece, the Stone Chariot, is not merely a sculpture but a profound engineering marvel of the 16th century. This challenge invites contributors to document the nuanced interplay of stone carvings and the symbolic geometry of the site.

We seek narratives that transcend standard tourism—entries that delve into the mythical origins of the Garuda shrine, the acoustic properties of the 'musical pillars', and the historical context of Krishnadevaraya's patronage. Your contribution helps us preserve the digital architectural footprint of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

The Creative Brief

This month, we invite our community to document the spaces where humanity has sought meaning. From austere modernist chapels to ancient monolithic ruins, capture the structural manifestations of faith, philosophy, and collective purpose. Submissions will be evaluated on narrative depth, technical mastery, and adherence to our minimalist editorial standards.

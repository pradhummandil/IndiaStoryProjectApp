Timeline: The Chola Dynasty

Trace the rise of the 'Imperial Cholas', one of the longest-ruling dynasties in world history, whose maritime influence spanned from the Ganges to Southeast Asia.

848 AD

Vijayalaya Chola's Revival

Vijayalaya Chola captured Thanjavur from the Muttarayar, laying the foundation for the Imperial Chola Empire.

985 AD

Accession of Rajaraja I

Rajaraja I 'The Great' ascends the throne, marking the beginning of the empire's golden age and military expansion.

1010 AD

Brihadisvara Temple

Completion of the massive Rajarajeswaram temple in Thanjavur, a marvel of Dravidian architecture and the world's first granite temple.

1025 AD

Maritime Srivijaya Campaign

Rajendra I launches a massive naval expedition across the Bay of Bengal to conquer Srivijaya (present-day Indonesia/Malaysia).
